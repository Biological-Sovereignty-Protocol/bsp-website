# BSP Taxonomy — Level 1: Core Longevity Biomarkers

> Version 0.2 | Ambrósio Institute

Advanced longevity and biological aging biomarkers. The scientific foundation of the BSP — biomarkers that measure the mechanisms of aging directly.

**Required for:** BSP-Compliant Advanced and Full-Spectrum certification.

---

## Category 01 — Longevity & Aging (BSP-LA)

The foundational category. These biomarkers measure aging mechanisms at the molecular level.

| BSP Code | Biomarker | Clinical Significance |
|---|---|---|
| `BSP-LA-001` | **GDF-11** | Growth Differentiation Factor 11 — circulating rejuvenation signal; declines with aging and correlates with regenerative capacity loss across organ systems |
| `BSP-LA-002` | **TIMP2** | Tissue Inhibitor of Metalloproteinase 2 — neural aging inhibitor identified in young plasma research at Stanford as a cognitive restoration factor |
| `BSP-LA-003` | **TELO** | Telomere Length — direct cellular aging indicator; validated in meta-analyses of >100,000 participants as predictor of all-cause mortality |
| `BSP-LA-004` | **NAD+** | Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide — metabolic energy and DNA repair; declines ~50% between ages 40–60, impairing sirtuin and mitochondrial function |
| `BSP-LA-005` | **MMP9** | Metalloproteinase 9 — marker of cellular inflammation, matrix remodeling, and tissue aging |

## Category 02 — Regeneration & Cellular Health (BSP-RC)

Monitors the metabolic, hormonal, and inflammatory axes that govern whether cells repair, grow, or enter senescence.

| BSP Code | Biomarker | Clinical Significance |
|---|---|---|
| `BSP-RC-001` | **HOMA-IR** | Homeostatic Model Assessment of Insulin Resistance — metabolic longevity predictor with strongest epidemiological validation globally |
| `BSP-RC-002` | **IGF-1** | Insulin-like Growth Factor 1 — regeneration and cellular growth; maintenance in optimal range reduces chronic inflammation |
| `BSP-RC-003` | **MTOR** | mTOR Pathway Activity — autophagy regulation, cellular growth, and elimination of damaged components |
| `BSP-RC-004` | **IL-6** | Interleukin 6 — primary marker of systemic inflammation and predictor of mortality in longitudinal studies |
| `BSP-RC-005` | **PCR** | C-Reactive Protein — systemic inflammation indicator and cardiovascular risk marker |

## Category 03 — Cardiovascular Health (BSP-CV)

Replaces conventional markers with biomarkers validated for superior predictive power in longevity medicine.

| BSP Code | Biomarker | Clinical Significance |
|---|---|---|
| `BSP-CV-001` | **APOB** | Apolipoprotein B — direct count of atherogenic particles; superior to LDL as cardiovascular risk predictor |
| `BSP-CV-002` | **HCIS** | Homocysteine — independent cardiovascular and neurodegenerative risk marker; associated with stroke and dementia |
| `BSP-CV-003` | **NITOX** | Endothelial Nitric Oxide — primary vascular vasodilator; decline indicates accelerated vascular aging |
| `BSP-CV-004` | **OMEGA3** | Omega-3 Index (EPA/DHA) — cardiovascular anti-inflammatory marker with extensive clinical trial support |

## Category 04 — Immune Function & Inflammation (BSP-IM)

Monitors "inflammaging" — the chronic low-grade inflammation that accelerates biological aging.

| BSP Code | Biomarker | Clinical Significance |
|---|---|---|
| `BSP-IM-001` | **VITD** | Vitamin D (25-OH) — immune system regulator; deficiency linked to accelerated biological aging across multiple organ systems |
| `BSP-IM-002` | **GLUTH** | Glutathione (GSH) — master antioxidant; declines with age and indicates oxidative stress burden |
| `BSP-IM-003` | **PCRHS** | High-Sensitivity CRP — sensitive inflammation marker; elevated levels predict biological aging acceleration |
| `BSP-IM-004` | **CD38** | CD38 (NADase) — enzyme that degrades NAD+; elevated activity is a marker of immune aging and NAD+ depletion |
| `BSP-IM-005` | **IMMAGE** | Immune Age Score — composite marker of immune system biological age derived from T-cell and B-cell population analysis |

Measures mitochondrial function and cellular energy production — the engine of biological vitality.

| `BSP-ME-004` | **LACTO** | Lactate — exercise recovery marker and metabolic stress indicator |

## Category 09 — Biological Clock & Senescence (BSP-BC)

Epigenetic aging and cellular senescence — the most direct measures of biological age.

| BSP Code | Biomarker | Clinical Significance |
|---|---|---|
| `BSP-BC-001` | **DNAM** | DNA Methylation Age (Epigenetic Clock) — Horvath/GrimAge/PhenoAge clocks; most validated measure of biological age |
| `BSP-BC-002` | **P16** | p16INK4a — senescent cell burden marker; elevated levels indicate accelerated cellular aging |
| `BSP-BC-003` | **P21** | p21 (CDKN1A) — cell cycle arrest and senescence marker |
| `BSP-BC-004` | **SASP** | SASP Factor Panel — Senescence-Associated Secretory Phenotype; composite of IL-6, IL-8, MMP3, PAI-1 |
