# Use Cases

BSP is infrastructure. These pages show what gets built on top of it.

## [Health Data Ownership](/use-cases/health-data-ownership)

Take permanent ownership of your blood tests, medical records, and wearable data. No gatekeepers. No expiration. Anchored on Arweave forever.

**Keywords:** health data ownership, own your medical records, health data sovereignty

---

## [Decentralized Health Records](/use-cases/decentralized-health-records)

Permanent, cryptographically verified health records that don't depend on any institution staying online. Built on Arweave + SmartWeave contracts.

**Keywords:** decentralized health records, blockchain health data, decentralized medical records

---

## [Open Health Data Standard](/use-cases/open-health-data-standard)

A developer-first open standard for biological data. Compare BSP vs FHIR, HL7, and proprietary APIs. Built for labs, clinics, and health platforms.

**Keywords:** open health data standard, health data interoperability, FHIR alternative

---

## [Longevity AI Data](/use-cases/longevity-ai-data)

Structured, typed, cryptographically verified biological data for longevity AI engines, biomarker analysis, and precision medicine models.

**Keywords:** longevity AI data, biomarker data AI, precision medicine data standard
